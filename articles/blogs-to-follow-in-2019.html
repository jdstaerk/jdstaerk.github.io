Blogs To Follow In 2019

February 22, 2019

There are tens of thousands of blogs and news portals. There are plenty of news aggregators like Google News. And each of them publishes and distribute blog posts every day. So what are the top sites to follow for interesting articles? Well, here are my favorites I consult on a regularly basis.

HackerNews: This is by far my #1 source for mostly tech news. It’s a news aggregator with a heavily used comment-section. It often provides great insights from professionals according to the topic of the shared article.

Stratechery: A great blog by Ben Thompson. According to his website, "Stratechery provides analysis of the strategy and business side of technology and media, and the impact of technology on society." Great in-depth articles for $10/mo (with one free weekly article).

Medium: I guess most of you know Medium. This self-publishing blog for everyone provides nice articles about nearly every topic - from politic to psychology and tech. Medium's so-called Top Stories are often worth a read.

Benedict Evans: He works for a VC firm in Silicon Valley and provides well-written articles about tech topics. He also sends out a weekly newsletter. His yearly presentations about economics and tech are also worth watching. You should definitely check his blog out!

ZDNet: ZDNet publishes (corporate) tech news ranging from mobile phones to cyber sec and cloud. Make sure to don’t procrastinate over there ;)
